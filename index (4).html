Clinical Data Operations Specialist – Health Informatics (Remote)

Location: Remote – U.S. Based (HQ: Denver, CO)

Employment Type: Full-Time

Travel Requirement: Up to 10%

Salary: $115,000 – $150,000/year

Company Overview

Meridian Health Analytics is a leading provider of healthcare data solutions supporting payers, providers, and health tech companies across the U.S. We specialize in medical coding compliance, risk adjustment analytics, population health reporting, and data integration services.

Position Summary

The Clinical Data Operations Specialist will ensure coding accuracy, documentation integrity, and regulatory compliance across various health plans. This role is fully remote and offers flexibility, growth, and mission-driven work.

Key Responsibilities

  • Retrospective and prospective reviews of medical records
  • Analyze documentation and claims for HCC/RxHCC models
  • Support coding initiatives for value-based programs
  • Collaborate to ensure adherence to guidelines
  • Assist with audits, documentation improvement
  • Interpret regulatory and coding updates
  • Support data integration and reporting projects

Qualifications

  • Bachelor's in HIM, Public Health, Nursing, or equivalent experience
  • Certified coder (CPC, CRC, CCS)
  • Experience in risk adjustment, clinical coding
  • Knowledge of ICD-10-CM, CPT/HCPCS, HEDIS, HCC models
  • Comfortable with EHRs and health data tools
  • Strong analytical and communication skills

Compensation & Benefits

  • Salary: $115k–$150k + performance bonus
  • Medical, dental, vision (Day 1)
  • 401(k) match, generous PTO, wellness days
  • Education reimbursement, remote work
  • Professional development & leadership training
